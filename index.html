Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at CSE, UC San Diego, advised by Professor Rajesh Gupta and Professor Jingbo Shang. My research focuses on developing machine learning algorithms that are more data-efficient, with applications in spatio-temporal modeling, natural language processing.

I received my B.S. degree in CS with honors from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University.
